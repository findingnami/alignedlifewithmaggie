# Aligned Life with Maggie

A personal branding and coaching website for **Julia Margaret**, a life and mindset coach serving Filipina professionals navigating career transitions. Built as a solo project — design, development, and deployment handled end to end.

🌐 **Live site:** [alignedlifewithmaggie.vercel.app](https://alignedlifewithmaggie.vercel.app)

---

## About the Project

Aligned Life with Maggie needed a web presence that felt as grounded and empathetic as the coach herself. The goal was to communicate warmth and credibility simultaneously — attracting women who are high-achieving but quietly burned out, and helping them take that first step toward booking a session.

The site positions Maggie's coaching practice at the intersection of career strategy and emotional well-being, with a specific focus on the Filipina professional experience.

---

## Features

- **Service showcase** — one-on-one coaching sessions, free discovery calls, and the *Career Shift* program
- **Client testimonials** — social proof from real clients across marketing, design, and education
- **Press section** — features from Cosmopolitan, Modern Filipina, and Smart Parenting
- **Newsletter signup** — integrated opt-in with an incentive offer
- **FAQ section** — addresses common coaching hesitations and objections
- **Social media integration** — links to a 10K+ Instagram following
- **Responsive design** — optimized for mobile and desktop

## Design Decisions

**Minimalist layout with warm photography** — The design deliberately steps back to let Maggie's imagery and copy do the heavy lifting. Coaching is an intimate service; the site needed to feel personal, not corporate.

**Single-page flow** — Rather than scattering content across multiple routes, the page guides visitors through a narrative arc: who Maggie is → what she offers → proof it works → how to get started.

**Conversion-focused structure** — Every section leads the visitor toward a clear next action, with multiple low-friction entry points (free call, newsletter, Instagram).
